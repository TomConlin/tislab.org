Jean-Phillipe (JP) Gourdine

Glycomics & Metabolomics Data Analyst

Oregon Health & Science University

ORCID

Overview

JP's research focuses on integration and analysis of glycomics & metabolomics data (molecular phenotypes) in correlation with genetic diseases. His work involves analysis of phenotypic, genomic, metabolomic & glycomic data from patients in the undiagnosed diseases network, as well as biocuration and ontology development.
