Overview

Shawn T. O'Neil (PhD, MS) serves as the Training Coordinator for N3C and Data Engineer for TISLab. Dr. O'Neil earned his BS in Computer Science at Northern Michigan University and later MS and PhD in the same subject at the University of Notre Dame, where he focused on sequence-based bioinformatics algorithms and uses. Shawn is the author of A Primer for Computational Biology with OSU Press and has developed and taught several courses and workshops in bioinformatics at Notre Dame and Oregon State University.
